This futuristic typeface allows the viewer to easily read the letterforms but also adds a unique geometric feature with its white fill ins. The fill ins create a bold element to the design that make it more eyecatching. This typeface would be great for headings, signage, or identity. The unique fill ins move the viewers eye through the entirety of the letterforms. The inspiration for this typeface references the cubist art movement where shapes were broken up into various sections creating a sense of movement.
